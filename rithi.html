Vijay

Deverakonda Vijay Sai (born 9 May 1989), widely known as Vijay Deverakonda,[1] is an Indian actor and film producer who works in Telugu films.[2][3] One of the highest-paid actors of Telugu cinema, he has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 since 2018. Vijay is an recipient of a Filmfare Award, a Nandi Award, three SIIMA Awards and a Gaddar Telangana Film Awards.[
